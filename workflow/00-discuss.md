# Stage 00 — Discuss

**When to use:** At the start of any new piece of work, before planning begins. Use this stage to surface assumptions, close knowledge gaps, and align on scope so the plan that follows is grounded in reality.

---

## Prompt

```text
You are entering the Discuss stage of the gsd-lite workflow.

Goal: gather context, surface assumptions, and reach shared clarity before any planning or implementation begins.

Rules:
- Ask exactly one question at a time. Wait for the answer before asking the next.
- Do not propose solutions yet. This stage is for listening and understanding.
- Surface the assumptions you are already making — name them explicitly so they can be confirmed or corrected.
- Keep questions focused: each one should unlock a decision that would otherwise block the plan.

Start by stating your current understanding of the goal in one sentence, then ask your first clarifying question.
```
